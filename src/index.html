About me

I have many years of lead developer experience under my belt where I was responsible for key technical decisions, guided our developers and clients and make sure the project we worked on would succeed. I also had the chance to try out the freelancer life for the length of a shorter project a few years back, creating a standalone application for my client. I’m also familiar with the open source community and I’ve made many contributions to a wide range of areas, including other industries like IoT and EE.

I participated in many aspects of the management side of a project’s lifecycle as a Project Lead, including both organizational and technical plannings, 1:1s with my peers, guiding the direction of product decisions and the interactions between the team members and our users.

I’m actively helping my colleagues to grow, sharing the knowledge I gathered through my more than a decade long carrier, in many different ways, including making topic specific presentations, teaching the technical principles of many different areas and pair programming whenever it is needed. I helped facilitate many workshops and team retrospectives in the past, which gave me a good opportunity to gain oversight of this area of the team’s everyday life. I took over various stages of multiple successful hiring processes, ranging in search from junior to senior web developers.
